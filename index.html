The vision

Shrouded in secrecy and emanating exclusivity, the Skull Society is a group of 7,777 Skulls with uniquely hand drawn traits by renowned Neo-Pop Artist Teodoru Badiu. 

This collection pays homage to “Gavroche”. Quite clever and imbued with street smarts, this lovable character reflects a piece of us all: a complex amalgamation of emotions on a trip through the human experience.

These aren’t your average skulls. Each art piece is a reflection of Teodoru's belief that we all can be pillars of hope regardless of the gruesomeness of reality.

THE #SKULLTAKEOVER

The Summoning

All members of our SKULLYLIST will have 6 hours to mint one SKULLTOONS NFT. After the 6 hours are over, anyone and everyone can mint on our Public Sale and each wallet can mint up to 3 NFTs. 77 hours after mint, holders will be able to summon their unique Gavroche and worship Theodoru's creation.

The First Exclusive SKULLGAMES

In these monthly games, holders of SKULLTOONS and partnered projects will compete in a series of riddles to gain access to an ultra-rare Victors Collection. With a combination of cryptography, steganography, and riddles, the SKULLGAMES will feature an Alternate Reality style competition where only the most dedicated participants will succeed.

The Invisible Hand

Theodoru is the first artist supported by The Artist Playhaus: an NFT launchpad led by the masses and fueled by artist empowerment. The SKULL society will participate in a DAO to decide which artist will be highlighted next, giving them the power to make the highest level decision of any NFT project. Furthermore, all Holders can get WL for future launches from the launchpad.

The Gathering

Our Holder-controlled DAO is put in place to give true power to our holders and their ideas. The DAO will be funded with a portion of the profit from the public mint as well as 100% of the profit from the Victor's collection. Every NFT holder will be able to vote on which artists will be highlighted in the Launchpad NFT Hub and determine the fate of SKULL society.

Toy Factory

Welcome to the SKULLTOONS Toy Factory, where your childhood dreams will become reality. Featuring various real-life collectibles from the SKULLTOONS collection, the Toy Factory will allow you to own a tangible piece of SKULLTOONS by Theodoru.

THE REQUISITION

Go fishing in our lava pool for your new NFT. All holders will get to claim new NFTs in the same aesthetic as those featured in the SKULLTOONS collection. The SKULL Society is still trying to figure out where these NFTs came from and how they ended up in the pool, only time will have the answer to these questions.

About the Artist

Teodoru Badiu is a renowned Neo Pop Artist who brings his visions to life through the exploration of an array of mixed media formats like 3D, vector, traditional drawings, collage, and photography. Even if his cheerful bizarre characters seem to be a little menacing at first glance, they are friendly in the end.

What is an NFT?

Simply put, an NFT is a digital asset that no one can forge. NFTs are stored on a blockchain and their ownership can easily be verified. To own an actual NFT you can’t just right-click and save an image, you need to unlock your crypto wallet (metamask, or any alternative) and show that the NFT exists in your wallet.
‍
If the NFT is in your wallet, you are eligible for access to certain groups, real-world events, and special deals. You can’t do this with a PNG. Think of an NFT as a ticket to enter a whole metaverse that is only just beginning.

What exactly will I get?

Minting a piece of the Skulltoons Gavroche collection will yield the holder one NFT featuring a uniquely generated skull. Rarity and trait ranking will be released shortly following public launch and reveal.
